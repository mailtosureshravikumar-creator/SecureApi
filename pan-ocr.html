What Is PAN OCR?

PAN OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is an intelligent document processing solution that automatically extracts and digitizes data from PAN cards. Using advanced OCR and validation algorithms, PAN OCR converts PAN card images into structured, machine-readable data.

This eliminates manual data entry, reduces errors, and accelerates verification workflows in high-volume onboarding environments where accuracy, speed, and compliance are critical.

How to Interpret PAN OCR Results

Extracted Data Fields

  • Auto-population of onboarding forms
  • Cross-verification with identity sources
  • Structured storage in CRM/compliance systems

Confidence Scores

  • High confidence: Data can be auto-approved
  • Medium confidence: May require soft validation
  • Low confidence: Recommended for manual review

This enables risk-based automation without compromising accuracy and helps reduce re-submission cycles.

Validation & Consistency Checks

  • PAN format compliance
  • Logical consistency of name and date fields
  • Card type alignment with PAN structure
