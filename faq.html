PAN-GST Link Verification

What is PAN-GST link verification?

It checks whether a PAN number is linked with an active GST registration issued by tax authorities.

Why is PAN-GST linkage important?

It ensures tax compliance, prevents fake GST registrations, and validates genuine businesses.

Who should use PAN-GST verification?

NBFCs, fintechs, enterprises, lenders, marketplaces, and compliance teams.

Is PAN-GST verification real-time?

Yes, results are fetched in near real time from authoritative sources.

Can this be automated using an API?

Yes, the service is fully API-driven and supports automation.

Does it work for inactive GST numbers?

Yes, it also indicates inactive or cancelled GST registrations.

Is manual documentation required?

No, the verification is completely digital.

How accurate is the verification?

Accuracy is high as data is sourced from official government records.

Can this reduce GST fraud?

Yes, it helps identify fake or mismatched PAN-GST records.

Is it suitable for bulk verification?

Yes, it supports high-volume and bulk verification use cases.
