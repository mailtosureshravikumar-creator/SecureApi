What is Company Name Search?

Company Name Search is a verification service that enables organizations to validate and retrieve structured information about a business entity using its registered or trade name. The service confirms legal existence, registration status, and core company profile for due diligence, onboarding, and compliance checks.

How the Search Works

Input Submission

  • User submits a registered name, trade name, or partial company name.

Database Matching

  • System searches authoritative registries and official records for matching entities.

Normalization & Matching Logic

  • Maps spelling variations, abbreviations, and legal suffixes (Pvt Ltd, LLP, Inc, Ltd).

Result Classification

  • Ranks matches by relevance and confidence, including similar-name entities.

Interpreting the Results

1. Company Identification

  • Registered legal name
  • Corporate Identification Number (CIN) or equivalent
  • Registration date

Interpretation: Confirms legal identity and official recognition.

2. Registration Status

  • Active
  • Inactive
  • Struck off
  • Amalgamated or dissolved

Interpretation: Active indicates legitimacy; inactive or struck-off indicates potential risk.

3. Entity Type

  • Private Limited
  • Public Limited
  • LLP
  • Partnership
  • Proprietorship

4. Registered Address & Jurisdiction

  • Official registered office address
  • State or jurisdiction of incorporation

5. Similar or Duplicate Name Matches

  • List of similar or closely matching company names

Significance of Company Name Search

  • Strengthens Due Diligence: Confirms counterparties before engagement.
  • Enhances Compliance: Supports KYC, AML, and KYB readiness.
  • Reduces Fraud: Detects fake, misleading, or impersonated company names.
  • Improves Data Accuracy: Keeps clean records across CRM, risk, and compliance systems.
  • Enables Better Decisions: Supports credit, onboarding, and procurement decisions.

In trust-critical environments, Company Name Search serves as a foundation for reliable business verification and compliant growth.
