What is PAN-GST Link Verification?

The PAN-GST Verification API enables real-time validation of Permanent Account Number details and securely retrieves associated Goods and Services Tax registration information. It establishes a verified linkage between an individual or business PAN and its corresponding GST records, ensuring legal authenticity, tax registration status, and business legitimacy. This API plays a critical role in KYC and KYB workflows by validating ownership, confirming business identity, and ensuring compliance with statutory and regulatory requirements.

What the PAN-GST Verification API Does

  • Verifies the authenticity and validity of a PAN.
  • Retrieves GST registrations linked to the PAN.
  • Confirms legal ownership and business association.
  • Validates GST status (Active, Inactive, Cancelled, Suspended).
  • Enables real-time compliance and risk assessment.

How It Works

Interpreting the Verification Results

The API response provides structured, actionable insights that can be easily interpreted and automated across systems.

  • PAN Validation Status: Valid (PAN exists and is active) or Invalid (PAN does not exist or is incorrectly entered).
  • PAN Holder Type: Individual, Proprietorship, Partnership, Private/Public Limited Company, LLP, or Trust.
  • GST Linkage Details: GSTINs associated with PAN, legal business name, trade name, jurisdiction, and registration state.
  • GST Registration Status: Active, Cancelled, Suspended, or Inactive.
  • Compliance Confidence Indicators: Business legitimacy, tax compliance readiness, and shell entity risk exposure.
