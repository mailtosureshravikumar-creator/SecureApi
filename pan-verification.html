What Is PAN Verification

PAN Verification is the process of validating a Permanent Account Number (PAN) issued by the Income Tax Department of India to confirm the authenticity of an individual or business entity.

The verification ensures that the PAN provided during onboarding or transactions is valid and active, matches official government records, and belongs to the correct individual or organization. PAN verification serves as a foundational step in KYC, tax compliance, and financial risk assessment workflows.

How PAN Verification Works

The PAN Verification API securely validates PAN details in real time by cross-checking the submitted PAN number against authorized government data sources.

The process delivers instant confirmation of PAN validity, holder name, PAN status, and PAN category (individual, company, firm, trust, etc.), enabling confident onboarding and compliance decisions.

Interpretation of PAN Verification Results

PAN Status

  • Valid & Active: PAN exists and is currently active.
  • Invalid: PAN does not exist or is incorrectly entered.
  • Inactive: PAN is deactivated, surrendered, or blocked.

Name Matching

  • Confirms whether provided name matches the official PAN record.
  • Supports full match, partial match, and mismatch indicators.
  • Helps detect identity discrepancies and impersonation attempts.

PAN Category

  • Individual
  • Company
  • Partnership Firm
  • Trust
  • Government Entity
