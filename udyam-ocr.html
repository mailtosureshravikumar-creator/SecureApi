What is Udyam Certificate OCR?

The Udyam (MSME) Extract API enables real-time verification of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprise (MSME) registration details directly from official Udyam registration records maintained by the Government of India. The API allows lenders, financial institutions, and enterprises to authenticate the legitimacy of a business by validating its registration status, enterprise classification, and ownership informationâ€”ensuring regulatory alignment and stronger risk assessment at the time of onboarding or credit evaluation.

What Information Does the API Extract?

The API securely retrieves verified MSME data, enabling accurate business verification and compliance validation.

  • Udyam Registration Number (URN)
  • Enterprise Name
  • Type of Enterprise (Micro / Small / Medium)
  • Registration Status (Active / Inactive)
  • Date of Registration
  • Business Activity Type (Manufacturing / Services)
  • Ownership / Proprietor or Entity Details
  • Registered Address
  • Linked PAN Details (where applicable)

How to Interpret the API Results

The API response provides structured insights that help teams understand Udyam registration outcomes and make informed onboarding and risk decisions.

1. Registration Status

  • Active: The enterprise is officially registered and valid under Udyam.
  • Inactive / Not Found: The business may not be registered, or the details provided are incorrect or outdated.
  • Significance: Confirms whether the business is legally recognized as an MSME.

2. Enterprise Classification

  • Micro / Small / Medium, based on investment and turnover thresholds defined by MSME regulations.
  • Significance: Determines eligibility for MSME-specific lending programs, pricing, credit policies, and government benefits.

3. Ownership & Entity Details

  • Validates the name of the proprietor, partner, or entity against official records.
  • Significance: Helps detect mismatches, impersonation risks, or misrepresentation during onboarding.

4. Business Activity Type

  • Manufacturing or Services classification.
  • Significance: Supports sector-specific risk analysis, underwriting logic, and policy alignment.

5. Registration Date & Continuity

  • Indicates how long the enterprise has been registered under Udyam.
  • Significance: Longer registration history often correlates with operational stability and lower risk.
